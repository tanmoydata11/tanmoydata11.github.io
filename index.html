A comprehensive Tableau HR Analytics Dashboard examining employee turnover patterns across 4,410 employees to understand who is leaving, why, and what can be done to improve retention. Identified an overall attrition rate of 16.12% with critical findings across departments, gender, age groups, and salary bands.

A comprehensive Microsoft Excel dashboard analysing mobile app usage behaviour across 700 users segmented by age group, gender, and behaviour class. Built interactive visualisations tracking app usage, screen time, data consumption, and app install patterns — with scatter plots confirming strong positive correlations between apps installed and usage hours.
